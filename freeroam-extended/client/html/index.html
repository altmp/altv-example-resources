1. F2: toggle HUD
  2. T / Enter: open chat
  3. /tp <1 to 22> (teleport)
  4. /model <modelName> (change your ped model)
  5. /veh <modelName> (spawn a vehicle)
  6. /clearvehicles (clear your vehicles)
  7. /tune <index> <value> (tune vehicle)
  8. /weapons (give yourself weapons)
  9. /addcomponent <name> (add weapon component)
  10. /removecomponent <name> (remove weapon component)
  11. /dm (Toggles respawning in death match zone)
